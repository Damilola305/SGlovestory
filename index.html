The day I met my wife was a Friday morning I'll never forget. I was hesitant to drive my mom to the hospital, but little did I know, it would lead me to my soulmate. I needed a pen to fill out a form, and that's when I saw her - a beautiful woman from my home state. We struck up a conversation, and I was smitten. My mom loved her too, and we all hit it off. As we talked more, I realized we shared the same values, goals, and sense of humor. The rest, as they say, is history. Today, I'm grateful for that chance encounter.
